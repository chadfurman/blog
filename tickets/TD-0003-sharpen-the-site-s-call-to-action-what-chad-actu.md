# TD-0003 · Sharpen the site's call-to-action — what Chad actually wants

## Why

"Available for Technical Leadership" is vague — it doesn't tell a visitor what
action to take or what Chad is actually looking for (Director/VP role? advisory?
fractional? board?). A clear ask gives the whole site a spine and a measurable
conversion path.

## What

Decide the primary intent, then make the conversion path obvious.

- Pick the concrete ask(s): full-time leadership, advisory/fractional, speaking,
  etc. (needs a decision from Chad).
- Rewrite the hero availability line + contact section to state it plainly.
- Make one primary CTA dominant (e.g. "Book a call" / "Email me") instead of
  equal-weight buttons; secondary links below.
- Optional: a scheduling link (Cal.com / Calendly) for the primary action.

## Acceptance

- [ ] Primary intent decided and reflected in hero + contact copy
- [ ] One dominant CTA with a clear next step; secondary actions de-emphasized
- [ ] Availability line states what Chad is open to, specifically
