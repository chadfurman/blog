Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between Essential, Professional, and Business?+

All tiers include hosting, backups, security, and updates. Professional adds email support, more support tokens, advanced speed optimization, and Core Web Vitals monitoring. Business adds text/call support, 8 tokens/mo, priority speed optimization, and monthly strategy check-ins.

What are premium support tokens?+

Each token covers one task under 30 minutes — content updates, plugin installs, CSS fixes, etc. Essential gets 2/mo, Professional 4/mo, Business 8/mo. Additional tokens available at $40–$50 each.

What if I already have hosting?+

We’ll migrate your site to our managed hosting at no extra cost. Migration is included with every plan.

What’s included in the monthly report?+

Uptime stats, page speed metrics, security scan results, update logs, and any recommendations for improvement.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Monthly plans have no contracts — cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Annual plans are billed upfront for the year.

Do you build new WordPress sites?+

Yes! Starter sites start at $300 when paired with an annual care plan, or $500 standalone. Custom builds are scoped individually.
