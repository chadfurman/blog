Frequently Asked Questions

WooCommerce or Shopify — which should I use?+

WooCommerce is best if you want full control and already have a WordPress site. Shopify is great if you want a simpler, hosted solution. We’ll help you choose based on your needs.

Can you migrate my store from another platform?+

Yes — we handle product, customer, and order data migration from most major platforms including Magento, BigCommerce, and Etsy.

What’s the difference between Store Care and Store Growth?+

Store Care ($249/mo) includes WordPress Care Professional, basic CRO monitoring, email support, and 4 support tokens/mo. Store Growth ($449/mo) adds active conversion optimization, text/call support, 8 tokens/mo, revenue analysis, and monthly strategy calls.

Is WordPress Care included?+

For WooCommerce stores, yes — hosting, security, updates, and backups are all included. For Shopify stores, you pay Shopify directly; our maintenance covers management and optimization.

What payment processors do you support?+

Stripe, PayPal, Square, and most major gateways. We’ll set up whatever works best for your business and customers.
