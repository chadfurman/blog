Frequently Asked Questions

How does performance-based pricing work?+

You pay a lower base fee ($250/mo annual) plus 5–10% of the revenue my marketing generates above your current baseline. I only get paid more when you make more. Prefer a flat rate? $500/mo annual covers everything.

Why does Marketing require a care plan?+

Effective SEO and email marketing need direct access to your site — for on-page optimization, plugin integration, landing pages, and tracking. A care plan ensures I have the access and infrastructure to deliver results.

How long before I see SEO results?+

SEO is a long game — most clients see meaningful ranking improvements within 3–6 months. We provide monthly reports so you can track progress from day one.

Do I need a Klaviyo account?+

Yes, but we’ll help you set it up. Klaviyo has a free tier for small lists. You pay Klaviyo directly — it’s not included in our fee.

What kind of email flows do you set up?+

Welcome series, abandoned cart recovery, post-purchase follow-ups, win-back campaigns, browse abandonment, and more — scoped to your needs during setup.

What’s the setup fee for?+

Marketing setup is substantial: SEO audit, Klaviyo integration, email flow creation, list setup, warming, and keyword research. Setup is $1,500 for annual clients or $3,000 for monthly clients.
